Pro Strategies

Hashtag Tips to Maximize Your Reach

01

Use a Mix of Hashtag Sizes

Combine mega hashtags (1M+ posts) with medium (100K–1M) and small niche tags (10K–100K). This maximizes both broad discovery and targeted reach where you can actually rank.

02

Research Platform-Specific Limits

Instagram allows up to 30 hashtags, TikTok works best with 5–10, Twitter recommends 1–3, and LinkedIn sees best results with 3–5 professional industry tags.

03

Rotate Your Hashtag Sets

Using the exact same hashtags repeatedly can reduce algorithmic reach. Generate multiple sets with our AI and rotate them across your posts to stay fresh.

04

Place Hashtags Strategically

On Instagram: first comment or end of caption. On TikTok: in the caption. On Twitter: inline within your tweet text for natural integration and higher click-through.

05

Track Performance and Iterate

Monitor which hashtag sets drive the most impressions and saves. Use insights from each platform to refine your AI prompts and generate even better results.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI Hashtag Generator work? +
Our tool uses Google's Gemini AI to analyze your content description, topic, and chosen platform. It then generates a strategically optimized set of hashtags that balances trending tags, niche-specific tags, and broad discovery tags — all calibrated for your specific platform's algorithm.
Is the AI Hashtag Generator completely free? +
Yes! Our tool is 100% free to use. You only need a Gemini API key from Google AI Studio, which also offers a generous free tier. Your API key is stored locally in your browser — we never store or share it.
How many hashtags should I use per platform? +
Best practices vary: Instagram allows up to 30 (we recommend 15–25 for best results), TikTok works best with 5–10 focused tags, Twitter/X recommends 1–3 hashtags, YouTube benefits from 5–15 SEO-rich tags, and LinkedIn performs best with 3–5 professional industry tags. Our AI automatically adjusts recommendations per platform.
Can I generate hashtags in languages other than English? +
Absolutely. Our AI supports 10+ languages including Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Hindi, Arabic, Japanese, Korean, and Italian. Select your preferred language in the advanced options and Gemini AI will generate culturally relevant hashtags for your target audience.
Is my API key safe? Do you store any data? +
Your Gemini API key is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage — it never leaves your device or gets sent to our servers. We don't collect, store, or process any personal data. All AI requests go directly from your browser to Google's Gemini API.
How often should I change my hashtag strategy? +
We recommend refreshing your hashtag sets every 2–4 weeks to adapt to platform trends. Use our regenerate feature to create new variations of hashtags for the same niche. Consistently rotating hashtag sets also prevents algorithmic shadowbanning that can occur from using identical tags every post.
What makes AI hashtags better than manual research? +
Manual hashtag research is time-consuming and often misses niche opportunities. Gemini AI instantly processes your content context, understands current platform trends, and generates a strategically diverse mix that would take hours to compile manually.
